Kara Jackson

An interactive web zine that honors how poets and their sonic counterparts folk singers, have the ability to speak for people in their time of rawness. Each quote is a lyric or stanza from their short yet impressive career as a National Youth Poet Laureate in 2019-2020 and thier debut album,Why Does the Earth Give Us People to Love?.
